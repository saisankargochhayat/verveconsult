Welcome to Verve About Us

Verve is a leading professional services firm, offering advisory services in Climate Change and Infrastructure. We have provided our advisory services to many government and non government clients. Verve has carried out approximately 50 CDM projects which required deep understanding of renewable energy and industrial processes and was amongst the market leaders in the climate change business. We have more than 50 business associates in various sectors. Our client list ranges from the top 100 companies of India to bilateral and multilateral funding organisations like World Bank, DFID, ADB, etc. and includes many state governments.
