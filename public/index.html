OUR SERVICES

We provide quality services in these three domains:

  • INFRASTRUCTURE ADVISORY
  • CLIMATE CHANGE ADVISORY
  • INCUBATIONAL SERVICES

INFRASTRUCTURE ADVISORY

Verve has been engaged for advising Government bodies, private corporations, projects developers and managers, as well as multilateral organizations for development and management of infrastructure projects across India. We specialize in providing innovative solutions for infrastructure projects for more effective mobilization of private sector and government resources. Verve had the opportunity to advise on diverse infrastructure and PPP transactions in the states of Odisha and Gujarat.
Our service offerings in infrastructure include:

  • Design of infrastructure policy
  • Mapping Infrastructure requirements
  • Facilitation of technology transfer
  • Feasibility and related studies
  • Configuring and facilitating Public Private Partnerships
  • Procurement management
  • Investment sourcing and implementation support
  • Potential JV/Partner Profiling
  • Entry strategy for new entrants
We also have a close working relationship with several local and international firms who can provide appropriate engineering consulting services to our clients.

CLIMATE CHANGE ADVISORY

Verve has been actively involved in developing CDM projects in India for the last 10 years. Over the years Verve has emerged as one of the market leaders in the Climate Change Business under the Clean Development Mechanism of the UNFCCC as well as Voluntary markets based on different Voluntary Standards. This advisory wing has assisted a number of corporates and PSUs with their CDM projects in the areas of Steel, Sugar, Fertilizers, Cement, Renewable Energy, Energy Efficiency, Energy Transmission and Distribution, Paper, Afforestation and Reforestation, etc.
Our primary areas of practice in climate change advisory include:

  • Identification, development and registration of Climate change projects
  • Investment sourcing for clean technology options
  • Technology identification and implementation support in transaction and risk Management in Carbon trading
  • Research on new technology and carbon markets
  • Capacity building in climate change management
